Cream Puffs

Ingredients

  • 2 (3.5 oz) Packages of Instant Vanilla Pudding Mix
  • 2 Cups of Heavy Cream
  • 1 Cup of Milk
  • 1/2 Cup of Butter
  • 1 Cup of Water
  • 1/4 Teaspoon of Salt
  • 1 Cup of All-Purpose Flour
  • 4 Eggs

Directions

  1. Mix together pudding mix, cream, and milk.
  2. Cover and refridgerate mixture to set.
  3. Preheat oven to 425 degrees Farenheit or 220 degrees Celsius.
  4. Boil water and butter in a large pot.
  5. Stir in flour and salt until the mixture forms a ball.
  6. Move the dough into a large mixing bowl.
  7. Using a wooden spoon or stand mixer, beat in eggs one at a time.
  8. Drop tablespoonfuls onto an ungreased baking sheet.
  9. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes, or until golden brown. Centers should be dry.
  10. When the shells are cool, split and fill them with the pudding mixture, or use a pastry bag to pipe the pudding into the shells.
