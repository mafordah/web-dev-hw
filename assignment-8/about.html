About

I chose to do an instructable on how to draw a dinosaur because it remind me of a project I had to do back it middle school for an extracurricular class. I've always been a firm believer that anyone can draw if they put their mind to it, and I love drawing for my little cousins and showing them how. Back in the day, I showed my class how to draw a cartoon elephant on the front whiteboard. For this go around, I chose a brontosaurus that I drew in Photoshop and took PNG's of it at different intervals.


Interested in drawing more dinosaurs? Visit HelloKids for more dinosaur drawing tutorials.
