Shel Silverstein

Sheldon Allan Silverstein, better known by his pen name, Shel Silverstein, was an American children's author, artist, lyricist, and poet. He was born in Chicago, Illinois, September 25th, 1930. He began drawing at age seven and had his first cartoon series Take Ten published in 1955. He was an accomplished artist with two Grammy awards and 20 million book copies sold. He died May 10th, 1999 at age 68.
